The heavy lift is complete — land control, entitlements, incentives, and early activation — creating a clear, de-risked path to monetization through land sales, building sales, joint ventures, and stabilized income.

The strategy is simple and proven: Assemble. Stabilize. Monetize. Each phase has been executed with discipline, and the platform is now positioned for significant vertical development upside.

The Sponsor

Future of Cities

Future of Cities is a real estate investment and development platform with over 60 years of combined executive experience. The team has led and invested in transformative neighborhood-scale and master-planned developments in Florida, including the Wynwood Arts District and Magic City Innovation District (Little Haiti).

Founded by Tony Cho, an early catalyst in emerging districts and founding board member of the Wynwood BID, Future of Cities pairs strong financial performance with place-based impact. Through public-private partnerships and a global advisory board of urban development and impact experts, the firm targets opportunity-driven investments with a focus on long-term value creation.
